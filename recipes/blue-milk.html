Blue Meanies

Description

A twist in a traditional margarita that is lightly sweetened but still packs a punch. Luke's time at Aunt Beru and Uncle Owen's farm might have been a lot more fun if he had some Blue Meanies instead of Blue Milk. Maybe he would have even made it to Toshie station to get some of those power converters...

Ingredients

  • Ice Cubes
  • Tequila - 2 fuild ounces
  • Triple Sec - 1 ounce
  • Lemon Juice - 0.5 fluid ounces
  • Lime Juice - 0.5 fluid ounces
  • Blue Curacao Liqueur - 0.5 fluid ounces

Steps

  1. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice cubes. Pour tequila, orange juice, triple sec, lemon juice, lime juice, and blue Curacao over the ice; shake. Strain into a pint glass filled with ice.
